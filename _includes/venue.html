The Vineyard is nestled in the Northeast Georgia mountains. Tiger mountain rises above the vineyard and casts a beautiful silhouette against the fall sun. Trees surround the venue and their autumn leaves will be beautiful reds, oranges and golds. The ceremony will be held in the field surrounding the large oak tree.
