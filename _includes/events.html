Wedding Events

Wedding and reception will take place at Tiger Mountain Vineyards in Tiger, Georgia. The ceremony will begin at 4 o'clock in the afternoon on the 15th of October, 2016, followed by cocktail hour with wine from the vineyard and cheese.

Barbeque dinner and beverages will be served by Williamson Brother's BBQ around 6:00 p.m. Guests of all ages are welcome to enjoy the festivities and lawn games. The Tiger Mountain Vineyard tasting room will be open for those intersted.
