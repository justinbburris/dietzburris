Bridesmaids and Groomsmen

Maid of Honor
Christina Dietz

Christina is Stephanie's favorite sister. When Stephanie was little, she wanted a sister more than anything, and prayed for one for 3 years. Stephanie and Christina are able to laugh at pretty much everything, and especially enjoy finding some of the most ridiculous clothing online. Christina is studying at Kennesaw State University, and has been dating her boyfriend Bennett for three years.

Bridesmaid
Anne Weber

Stephanie usually introduces Anne as her "technical" aunt. Anne and her husband Dave live in Florida, where Anne works as a medical trainer. Even though Anne and Stephanie didn't live together, Anne took the time to teach Stephanie important things, like makeup, good music, and not to take high school drama too seriously. Anne has two dogs, Batman and Bubba, and enjoys traveling and listening to audio books.

Bridesmaid

Stephanie and Michela met through mutual friends during college, where they bonded over a love of food and dancing. Michela and Stephanie both love to try new things, and enjoy dinner parties. Michela currently lives in North Carolina, where she is finishing her masters degree.

Bridesmaid
